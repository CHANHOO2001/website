About / 关于我

我目前就读于西北农林科技大学马克思主义学院，专业为马克思主义理论。 我的研究兴趣包括思想政治教育、马克思主义理论、法治教育，以及人工智能在教育中的应用。

I am a master's student in Marxist Theory at the School of Marxism, Northwest A&F University. My academic interests include ideological and political education, Marxist theory, rule of law education, and the application of artificial intelligence in education.

This website serves as a personal academic homepage and a long-term space for research notes, reading reflections, teaching observations, travel writing, and everyday essays.
